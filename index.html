Planning Committee and Responsibilities

Internment Unit

  • Casket
  • Grave preparations (Land, digging, cementing, design)
  • Corpse logistics (ambulances)
  • Undertakers (casket bearers)
  • Clothing the corpse
  • Table and decorations arrangements for the service of songs
  • Interface with St. John Anglican Church, Ogbagi, and Christian Association of Nigeria (CAN)

Reception and Entertainment Unit

  • Estimate the total number of people we are feeding.
  • Determine the number of cows and chickens for meat.
  • Calculate the quantities of rice, semo vita, beans, salad ingredients, spices, etc.
  • Ensure we have all necessary items like pots, plates, take-away plates, spoons, cups, and other cutlery. Consider renting these ourselves instead of leaving them for the hired caterer, to save costs(my opinion).
  • Estimate the gallons of red oil and vegetable oil needed for all days.
  • Hire ushers and decoration people.
  • Hire catering people for cooking.
  • Discuss with and hire music artists for the reception/occasion.
  • Hire trumpet and drums (Boys Brigades) for mobilization music and dance.
  • Estimate the financial budget for drinks and the cooling system for the event, including water, soft drinks, wines, cooling trucks, etc.
  • Plan, implement, and enforce food and drink sharing for the ceremony to ensure a great experience.
